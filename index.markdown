<section class="section-block" id="about-me">
  <p class="eyebrow">About me</p>
  <h2>Hi, I'm Aure — Aureliano Guedes.</h2>
  <p>I build Industrial GenAI and ML embedded directly in operations, shaping process decisions, engineering workflows, and industrial intelligence where it matters.</p>
  <p>At Acelen, I define and drive the industrial AI roadmap and lead Acel-IA, an enterprise GenAI platform that connects operational and engineering context into an intelligence layer for natural-language analytics, APC/MPC support, P&ID contextualization with live data, trip and abnormal event investigation, and multivariable decision support.</p>
  <p>My path here wasn’t linear. I started in bioinformatics and bioenergy research, working with plant genomics, transcriptomics, scientific databases, and predictive models. Later, I moved into ML for insurance and the energy sector, and eventually into industrial AI at scale. That early work shaped how I approach domain-heavy systems: refinery data isn’t that different from a genome — dense, context-dependent, and unforgiving when misinterpreted.</p>
  <p>These diverse experiences have convinced me that knowledge is best shared. That’s why I created this blog: to provide clear, actionable insights and examples that make data science and engineering approachable, practical, and useful.</p>
</section>
